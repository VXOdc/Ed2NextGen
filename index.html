Our Mission

Every student has something valuable to teach.

We believe that by creating free educational resources, student volunteers make learning more accessible while developing leadership, communication, and creativity. Our library grows one contribution at a time — reviewed, published, and shared with the world.

Why we started

Too many classrooms lack quality learning materials. Ed2NextGen connects young creators with communities that need them — no paywalls, no barriers, just learning that reaches everyone.

How It Works

From idea to published resource in four steps

A simple path for students who want to make a real difference.

Apply

Complete the volunteer application and tell us about your interests and skills.

Create

Write or design an educational resource using our submission guidelines.

Review

Our team reviews your work for quality, accuracy, and educational value.

Publish

Your resource becomes part of our free online library for anyone to download.
